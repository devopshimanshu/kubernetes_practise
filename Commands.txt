ClusterIp:
Its a kind of service in which if pod is exposed then within the cluster we can access pod with that IP and port But inside the cluster only not outside.
kubectl expose pod <podName> --port <containerPort> --target-port <clusterPort>

In clusterIp pod port is bind with clusterIp

In NordPort pod port is bind with workerNode Ip 
NodePort is accessable to outside world 
it has a security breach we are exposing the pod ports directly to the world 
to overcome this issue we have LoadBalancer 

For NodePort the port range is 30000 to 32767 port number
